About Jayashankar Bhupalpally District

Jayashankar Bhupalpally district is one of the forest-rich districts of Telangana. It was formed on October 11, 2016, after the reorganization of districts in the state. The district is named after Professor K. Jayashankar, a prominent Telangana ideologist.

The district is covered with dense forests, rivers, waterfalls, and mineral resources. A large part of the district lies under the Godavari river basin. It plays an important role in eco-tourism and tribal culture preservation.

Bhupalpally district is home to several tribal communities who follow traditional lifestyles. Agriculture, forestry, and small-scale industries are the major occupations. The region is also rich in historical heritage from the Kakatiya dynasty.

The district headquarters is located in Bhupalpally town. Due to its natural beauty and historical importance, the district is emerging as a major tourism destination in Telangana.

History of Bhupalpally District

12th – 13th Century:
Region flourished under the Kakatiya dynasty. Major constructions like Ramappa Temple were built.


Medieval Period:
Area came under different rulers including Qutb Shahi and Asaf Jahi dynasties.


1948:
Integrated into Indian Union after Hyderabad State merger.


2016:
Officially formed as Jayashankar Bhupalpally district during Telangana district reorganization.

Major Tourism Places

Laknavaram Lake

Laknavaram Lake was developed during the Kakatiya dynasty period. It is surrounded by lush green forests and hills. The suspension bridge connecting islands makes it one of the most scenic tourist attractions in Telangana.

Ramappa Temple

Ramappa Temple, built in 1213 CE during the Kakatiya rule, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is famous for its floating bricks and detailed carvings. The temple showcases advanced engineering skills of ancient India.

Bogatha Waterfall

Bogatha Waterfall is known as the Niagara of Telangana. It is located in dense forest region of Bhupalpally district. The waterfall becomes very powerful during monsoon season.

Yamanpalli Village

Yamanpalli village is located in Jayashankar Bhupalpally district. It represents traditional Telangana rural lifestyle. The village is surrounded by agricultural lands and natural greenery.
